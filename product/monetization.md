# Monetization Strategy

## 1. Objective

Build a sustainable, enterprise-grade revenue model for **SamvidAI** that balances early adoption with long-term scalability, trust, and compliance requirements.

---

## 2. Monetization Principles

* **Trust-first**: No hidden data usage or model training on client data
* **Value-based pricing**: Charge for outcomes, not just features
* **Land & expand**: Start small, grow within organizations
* **Enterprise-ready**: Support procurement, security, and compliance needs

---

## 3. Target Revenue Segments

### Primary

* Mid-to-large law firms
* In-house legal & compliance teams

### Secondary

* Legal consultants
* Contract-heavy enterprises (finance, infra, SaaS)
* Legal BPO / KPO firms

---

## 4. Pricing Model Overview

SamvidAI follows a **hybrid SaaS + enterprise licensing model**.

### Pricing Axes

* Number of users (seats)
* Monthly document volume
* Advanced features (analytics, integrations)
* Deployment model (cloud vs on‑prem)

---

## 5. Plans & Packaging

### 5.1 Pilot / Trial (Pre‑PMF)

* Invite-only
* Limited documents & users
* Free or nominal fee
* NDA + feedback agreement

**Goal:** Validate PMF and collect case studies

---

### 5.2 Starter (SMB / Small Legal Teams)

* Per-user / month pricing
* Cloud-hosted
* Core clause extraction & risk flags
* Basic exports

**Target:** Small law firms, startups

---

### 5.3 Team / Pro

* Per-seat + document volume
* Collaboration features
* Advanced clause libraries
* Priority support

**Target:** Growing legal teams

---

### 5.4 Enterprise

* Annual contracts
* Unlimited users (tiered)
* Custom document limits
* On‑prem / private cloud
* SSO, audit logs, SLA

**Target:** Large enterprises & regulated industries

---

## 6. Add‑On Revenue Streams

* Custom clause models (jurisdiction / domain-specific)
* API access
* Workflow integrations (DMS, ERP)
* Custom analytics dashboards
* Dedicated support & training

---

## 7. Proof‑of‑Value (POV) Projects

* 2–4 week paid pilots
* Fixed pricing
* Clear success metrics (accuracy, time saved)

**Outcome:** Faster enterprise sales conversion

---

## 8. Upsell & Expansion Strategy

* More users within the same org
* Higher document limits
* Advanced analytics modules
* Cross-team deployment (legal → compliance → procurement)

---

## 9. Cost Structure Awareness

Key cost drivers:

* LLM inference
* OCR & OpticalRAG pipelines
* Secure storage
* Enterprise support

Pricing is designed to maintain healthy gross margins while scaling.

---

## 10. Competitive Positioning

| Type                  | Typical Model  | SamvidAI |
| --------------------- | -------------- | -------- |
| Generic AI tools      | Per-token      | ❌        |
| Legacy legal software | Flat licenses  | ⚠️       |
| SamvidAI              | Value + volume | ✅        |

---

## 11. Metrics to Track

* ARPA (Avg Revenue Per Account)
* Gross margin
* Expansion revenue
* Churn (logo & revenue)
* Sales cycle length

---

## 12. Long‑Term Monetization Opportunities

* Industry-specific solutions (banking, infra, pharma)
* Regulatory intelligence modules
* Continuous compliance monitoring
* White-label offerings

---

## 13. Monetization Risks & Mitigation

| Risk                | Mitigation   |
| ------------------- | ------------ |
| Price sensitivity   | Tiered plans |
| Long sales cycles   | POV projects |
| Compute cost spikes | Usage caps   |

---

## 14. Alignment with Product Roadmap

* MVP → Core SaaS plans
* PMF → Team & Enterprise
* Scale → Add-ons & vertical solutions

---

*This monetization strategy is designed to evolve with customer maturity and regulatory expectations.*
