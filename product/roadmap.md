# Product Roadmap

## Vision

Build **SamvidAI** into a trusted, enterprise-grade legal intelligence platform that goes beyond contract review into continuous legal and compliance intelligence.

---

## Guiding Principles

* Solve **real legal workflows**, not demos
* Prioritize **accuracy, explainability, and trust**
* Ship incrementally with pilot feedback
* Design for **enterprise scale from day one**

---

## Phase 0 – Foundation (Completed / In Progress)

**Goal:** Strong technical and documentation base

* Repository structure & standards
* HLD / LLD documentation
* OpticalRAG pipeline design
* Data models & schemas
* GTM & Monetization strategy docs

---

## Phase 1 – MVP & Pilot (Q1)

**Goal:** Validate product–market fit

### Features

* PDF / DOCX ingestion
* OCR + OpticalRAG pipeline
* Core clause extraction

  * Termination
  * Indemnity
  * Liability
  * Governing law
* Risk flags with explanations
* Basic web UI
* Exportable summaries

### Outcomes

* 3–5 pilot users
* Qualitative feedback from legal professionals
* Accuracy & latency benchmarks

---

## Phase 2 – Paid Teams & Stability (Q2)

**Goal:** Move from pilots to revenue

### Features

* Multi-user collaboration
* Clause library management
* Improved explainability & citations
* Usage analytics
* Role-based access control

### Outcomes

* First paying customers
* Documented case studies
* Improved onboarding flow

---

## Phase 3 – Enterprise Readiness (Q3)

**Goal:** Close enterprise contracts

### Features

* On‑prem / private cloud deployment
* SSO & audit logs
* SLA & monitoring
* API access
* Integrations (DMS, workflows)

### Outcomes

* Enterprise pilots
* Annual contracts
* Security & compliance validation

---

## Phase 4 – Expansion & Intelligence (Q4)

**Goal:** Move beyond contract review

### Features

* Cross-contract analytics
* Regulatory & compliance intelligence
* Industry-specific clause models
* Continuous monitoring & alerts

### Outcomes

* Higher ACV
* Expansion revenue
* Strong differentiation

---

## Long-Term Opportunities

* Vertical solutions (banking, infra, pharma)
* White-label platform
* Legal knowledge graph
* AI-assisted negotiation support

---

## Success Metrics

* Adoption & retention
* Accuracy vs human review
* Revenue growth
* Enterprise expansion rate

---

*This roadmap is a living document and will evolve based on user feedback and market signals.*
