# User Personas

## Purpose

This document defines the core user personas for **SamvidAI**. These personas guide product design, feature prioritization, UX decisions, and go‑to‑market strategy.

---

## Persona 1: In‑House Legal Counsel

### Profile

* Role: Legal Counsel / Senior Legal Manager
* Organization: Mid‑to‑large enterprise
* Experience: 5–15 years

### Goals

* Review contracts quickly and accurately
* Identify legal risks early
* Maintain compliance with company policies
* Provide clear advice to business teams

### Pain Points

* High contract volume
* Limited time for deep reviews
* Inconsistent contract language
* Pressure to reduce turnaround time

### How SamvidAI Helps

* Automated clause extraction
* Risk flags with explanations
* Fast summaries for business stakeholders
* Centralized contract intelligence

---

## Persona 2: Law Firm Associate

### Profile

* Role: Associate / Senior Associate
* Organization: Corporate law firm
* Experience: 1–6 years

### Goals

* Review contracts efficiently
* Avoid missing critical clauses
* Deliver high‑quality work under time pressure

### Pain Points

* Manual clause scanning
* Repetitive contract reviews
* Long working hours
* High error risk

### How SamvidAI Helps

* Pre‑highlighted critical clauses
* Structured risk summaries
* Faster first‑pass review
* Reduced fatigue and errors

---

## Persona 3: Law Firm Partner

### Profile

* Role: Partner
* Organization: Law firm
* Experience: 10+ years

### Goals

* Ensure quality and consistency
* Scale firm capacity without linear hiring
* Increase profitability

### Pain Points

* Review bottlenecks
* Quality variance across associates
* Margin pressure

### How SamvidAI Helps

* Consistent clause detection
* Faster review cycles
* Improved leverage of junior staff

---

## Persona 4: Compliance & Risk Manager

### Profile

* Role: Compliance Manager / Risk Officer
* Organization: Regulated enterprise
* Experience: 6–15 years

### Goals

* Ensure regulatory compliance
* Monitor contractual risk exposure
* Support audits

### Pain Points

* Limited visibility across contracts
* Manual compliance checks
* Audit stress

### How SamvidAI Helps

* Compliance‑focused risk flags
* Cross‑contract insights
* Explainable AI outputs for audits

---

## Persona 5: Legal Operations Manager

### Profile

* Role: Legal Ops / Process Manager
* Organization: Enterprise legal department
* Experience: 4–10 years

### Goals

* Improve efficiency of legal workflows
* Track performance metrics
* Reduce operational costs

### Pain Points

* Poor tooling visibility
* Manual reporting
* Difficulty measuring ROI

### How SamvidAI Helps

* Usage & productivity analytics
* Standardized review workflows
* Measurable time savings

---

1. In‑House Legal Counsel
2. Law Firm Associates
